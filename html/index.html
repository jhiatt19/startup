This will be a place holder for my PDF extractor

I really have a difficult time reading. If I need to read for longer than 5 minutes at one time, I tend to zone out and am unable to focus as well. However, listening to longer articles or books is no big deal. So I created a python script to extract text out of a pdf so that I can have an AI read it to me.
